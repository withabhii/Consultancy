  • Tax saving strategy advisory: Maximizing deductions under sections like 80C, 10AA, etc.

GST Advisory & Services

Navigate the complexities of GST with our expert advisory services. From registration to return filing and audit representation, we provide end-to-end GST compliance solutions for businesses of all sizes.

Key Services:

  • GST registration: New registration, amendments, and cancellations
  • Monthly/Quarterly GST returns: GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, GSTR-9 annual returns
  • GST audits: Handling GST audit notices and representing before authorities
  • GST reconciliation: Matching GSTR-2A/2B with your books of accounts
  • E-invoicing setup: Implementation and compliance with e-invoicing requirements
  • LUT filing & export advisory: For businesses engaged in exports
  • GST refunds: Assistance in claiming input tax credit refunds

Benefits:

  • Timely compliance to avoid penalties
  • Maximization of input tax credit claims
  • Proper documentation for GST audits
  • Expert representation during GST inspections

Audit & Assurance Services

Our audit services provide independent verification of your financial statements, giving stakeholders confidence in your reported financial position. We go beyond compliance to identify opportunities for improving your financial processes and controls.

Key Services:

  • Statutory audit: Mandatory audit under Companies Act for companies
  • Internal audit: Independent evaluation of internal controls and processes
  • Tax audit (u/s 44AB): For businesses exceeding specified turnover limits
  • Stock audit / Inventory audit: Physical verification and valuation of inventory
  • Due diligence audit: For mergers, acquisitions, or investments
  • Forensic audits: Limited scope investigations for fraud detection
  • Bank audit: Statutory audit of banking companies

Benefits:

  • Compliance with statutory requirements
  • Identification of control weaknesses and fraud risks
  • Improved financial reporting accuracy
  • Enhanced credibility with investors and lenders
  • Operational efficiency recommendations
