Key Compliance Requirements

Essential corporate filings and their deadlines

Compliance Applicability Frequency Deadline
Annual Return (MGT-7/7A) All Companies Annual Within 60 days of AGM
Financial Statements (AOC-4) All Companies Annual Within 30 days of AGM
Board Meeting Minutes All Companies Quarterly (Min. 4 per year) Within 30 days of meeting
DIR-3 KYC All Directors with DIN Annual 30th September
MSME Form I (Half Yearly Return) Specified Companies with dues to MSMEs Half-yearly 30th April & 31st October
Secretarial Audit Report (MR-3) Listed Co. / Specified Public/Pvt Co. Annual With Annual Return

Our comprehensive accounting services ensure your financial records are accurate, up-to-date, and compliant with all regulatory requirements. We provide end-to-end solutions tailored to your business needs.

Company Incorporation & ROC

End-to-end support for business registration and ongoing statutory compliance with the Registrar of Companies (ROC).

  • Private Limited Company Incorporation: Complete assistance with name approval, DIN allocation, MOA/AOA drafting, and registration.
  • LLP Formation: Registration of Limited Liability Partnerships including partnership agreement drafting.
  • ROC Annual Filings: Preparation and submission of MGT-7 (Annual Return), AOC-4 (Financial Statements), and other mandatory forms.
  • Director KYC (DIR-3 KYC): Annual verification process for all directors to maintain active DIN status.
  • Shareholding Structure Setup: Assistance with share allotment, transfer, and maintenance of statutory registers.
  • Strike-off Services: Voluntary closure of dormant companies with all necessary ROC filings.

We handle all legal formalities so you can focus on building your business.
